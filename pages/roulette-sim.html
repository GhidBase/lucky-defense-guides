Roulette Simulator

How does this simulator work?

This Roulette Simulator was built with finding the best use for your luck stones. If you only want to get the most legendaries with as few luck stones as possible, what is the most cost effective way to spend luck stones?

Your options are:

  • Rare Roulette Spin (60%)
  • Epic Roulette Spin (35%)
  • Legendary Roulette Spin (14.2%)

(these assume artifact level 6 for simplicity)

Artifacts that affect your spins:

There are a couple artifacts that change the effectiveness of each type of roulette spin.

  • Skull Stone - Has a 15% chance to refund the cost when roulette summon fails
  • Gamblers Wrist - Has 15% chance to gain lower tier guardian when roulette summon fails
  • Horn - Increases epic summon chance by 7.5% in the roulette summon
  • Golden Dice - Increases legendary summon chance by 4.2% in the roulette summon
  • Receipt - Gains +1 luck stone by 4.5% when selling a guardian

Roulette Spin Strategies

There isn't much strategy at play with roulette spins other than using the most efficient one. There is one thing worth considering though: What do you do when you get a lower rarity guardian from a failed spin, with the Gambler's Wrist artifact? Do you save them to merge later? Or do you sell them? You would also need to consider that when you sell a guardian, you have a 4.5% chance to gain an extra luck stone from the Receipt artifact, on top of the luck stone(s) you normally get from selling. It's also worth considering that when your board starts to run out of space, you won't be able to save units for merging anymore, and regardless of the strategy that's technically most efficient, you will have to switch to selling your Gambler's Wrist guardians so you can have space and stones to keep doing your roulette spins.
