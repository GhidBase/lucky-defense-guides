The Age Old Question - 1x or 10x?

This is the Lucky Defense Recruitment Simulator. Do you ever wonder whats best for you? Should you do 1x summon or 10x summon? What resource are you trying to get the most of first? Do you want Mythic Stones, or are you still trying to get your Bandit to level 6 or level 12?

This tool will help you figure that out. Just enter the amount of Recruitment Scrolls you plan on spending, or how many of a specific resource you want to have before the simulator finishes running. Once the simulator is done, it'll show you how long it took to reach your goal, and what you could have gotten from those pulls overall.
