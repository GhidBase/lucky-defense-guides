Mythic roles

While mythics are extremely strong units, more mythics does not always mean easier wins. Every mythic in the game serves one or more roles. This is a quick overview of those roles.

Wave Carries

These are the most popular mythics in the game because their strengths are immediately obvious as soon as you get them. These are the wave carries:

  • Batman
  • Rocket Chu
  • King Dian
  • Dragon
  • Iron Meow
  • Lazy Taoist
  • Blob (lvl 6)

These are roughly in order of what is the most common. Side note, with multiple Lancelots (2 or 3) with a very high mp regen (400% mp regen or higher), Lancelot also becomes a wave clear option, and one that is particularly good in higher waves. Pair with a ranged wave clear such as Rocket Chu, King Dian, etc, and wave clearing should be very easy on hard mode.

Boss Killers/Dungeon Clearers

These are the most overlooked mythics because their benefits aren't immediately obvious. These are the boss killers:

  • Lancelot (lvl 6)
  • Vayne (lvl 6)
  • King Dian
  • Rocket Chu (lvl 12)
  • Lazy Taoist

A quick overview of these characters:

Note: Lancelot will NOT clear dungeon

Lancelot has a passive skill, where every hit he lands deals a percentage of the enemy's health in damage. This is an okay ability at first. But when he's level 6 he unlocks his ult, where he performs a slam attack that creates a cyclone. This cyclone deals rapid fire damage, and each does the normal amount of percent damage. If you have 2 or 3 level 6 Lancelots, and a couple level 12 Kitty Mage's, hard mode is very simple to clear. You do have to upgrade Lancelot's passive skill mid-match using lucky stones to get him dealing percent damage. Max this out in the late game and it's an easy win.

Vayne is one of the biggest sleeper units in the game. He does next to no damage on waves, if you have Vayne he will NOT help you stay alive during waves. This is a big drawback. However, Vayne will easily clear dungeon for you. If you have a decent amount of gold and mythic damage upgrades, he can even be useful killing bosses all the wave through wave 80 on hard. When using Vayne, it's totally fine to just move him out of range during waves so you can set up a better wave clear board, and then just move Vayne into range when the boss comes out.

King Dian and Rocket Chu are very interesting characters. They serve very similar roles, and both have an all or nothing gamble to make them powerful. I would personally say they are NOT boss killers. However, oftentimes when you have 2 level 6 Lancelots, you will need just a little more damage to defeat the wave 80 boss. More damage than Batman or Iron Meow could do. In this situation, King Dian and Overclocked Chu can likely finish the boss off. For that to happen, you will need to upgrade mythic damage and have a good stockpile of gold (at the very least 100k or higher) but they can definetly make the difference between a win or a loss in a pinch.
