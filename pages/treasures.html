List of Exclusive Treasures

These are all the unique treasures that only their specific mythic can unlock:

Treasure Level Description
Ninja Level 1 Increase Ninjutsu's skill DMG by 10%. Reduces CD by 1%.
MAX Increase Ninjutsu's skill DMG by 100%. Reduces CD by 25%.
Iron Meow Level 1 Increases Iron Meow v2's skill DMG by 5%. Increases CRIT rate by 1%.
MAX Increases Iron Meow v2's skill DMG by 50%. Increases CRIT rate by 10%.
Vayne Level 1 Deals 50% ATK DMG to nearby enemy upon enemy kill. DMG increases by 1% for 3 seconds after moving.
MAX Deals 200% ATK DMG to nearby enemy upon enemy kill. DMG increases by 25% for 3 seconds after moving.
Watt Level 1 5% energy returned after all energy is used. Increases ATK SPD by 1%.
MAX 50% energy returned after all energy is used. Increases ATK SPD by 10%.
Dragon Level 1 Increases Flame DMG by 5% after using Drain's skill. Increases skill DMG by 1%.
MAX Increases Flame DMG by 50% after using Drain's skill. Increases skill DMG by 20%.
Lazy Taoist Level 1 Increases Razorwind duration by 0.1 secs. Increases ATK SPD by 1%.
Max Level Increases Razorwind duration by 0.5 secs. Increases ATK SPD by 10%.
Frog Level 1 5% chance to refund 1 random material on each failed curse lift. Increases ULT Damage by 1%.
MAX 70% chance to refund 1 random material on each failed curse lift. Increases ULT Damage by 25%.
Tar Level 1 1% chance to summon a tier 3 Tar when summoning Tar that is not tier 3. Increases skill Activation Chance by 0.1%.
MAX 30% chance to summon a tier 3 Tar when summoning Tar that is not tier 3. Increases skill Activation Chance by 4%.
Mama Level 1 1% chance to summon an imp upon kill with Fear Skill. Increases skill DMG by 1%.
MAX 30% chance to summon an imp upon kill with Fear Skill. Increases skill DMG by 20%.
Bomba Level 1 Increases Bomba's basic attack DMG by 5%. Increases physical DMG by 1%.
MAX Increases Bomba's basic attack DMG by 70%. Increases physical DMG by 20%.
Kitty Mage Level 1 Increases magic DMG by 1% for 3 seconds after mana recovery. Increases skill Activation Chance by 0.1%.
MAX Increases magic DMG by 15% for 3 seconds after mana recovery. Increases skill Activation Chance by 5%.
Batman Level 1 Increases DMG by 1% for 10 seconds upon successful upgrade. Increases CRIT rate by 1%.
MAX Increases DMG by 30% for 10 seconds upon successful upgrade. Increases CRIT rate by 10%.
Monopoly Man Level 1 Gains 0.1% of the used MP after an ULT. Recovers 1% of MP at the start of a wave.
MAX Gains 5% of the used MP after an ULT. Recovers 20% of MP at the start of a wave.
Blob Level 1 Increases Devour Count by 5 upon Boss Kill. Increases ATK by 1%.
MAX Increases Devour Count by 50 upon Boss Kill. Increases ATK by 20%.
Indy Level 1 1% chance to upgrade to a higher-tier treasure upon excavation. Increases MOV SPD by 5%.
MAX 30% chance to upgrade to a higher-tier treasure upon excavation. Increases MOV SPD by 80%.
Lancelot Level 1 Summon Lancelot with 1 Flame level. Increases MP regen by 1%.
MAX Summon Lancelot with 10 Flame level. Increases MP regen by 30%.
Pulse Generator Level 1 Decreases MOV SPD during Space-Time Control by 5%. Increases ULT Damage by 1%.
MAX Decreases MOV SPD during Space-Time Control by 50%. Increases ULT Damage by 25%.
Orc Shaman Level 1 Increases physical DMG taken by curse-stunned enemies by 1% for 3 seconds. Increases skill Activation Chance by 0.1%.
MAX Increases physical DMG taken by curse-stunned enemies by 18% for 8 seconds. Increases skill Activation Chance by 5%.
Coldy Level 1 Increases number of Glaciers for Glacier Collision by 3. Increases MP regen by 1%.
MAX Increases number of Glaciers for Glacier Collision by 20. Increases MP regen by 30%.
Gravitron Level 1 Increases Gravity Control DMG by 10%. Increases skill Activation Chance by 0.1%.
MAX Increases Gravity Control DMG by 100%. Increases skill Activation Chance by 5%.
Zap Level 1 Increases damage by 10% when attacking the same target with the Arcane Amplification skill. Basic attack damage increases by 1%.
MAX Increases damage by 100% when attacking the same target with the Arcane Amplification skill. Basic attack damage increases by 25%.
Rocket Chu Level 1 1% chance to avoid explosion on Overclock failure. Reduces the number of attacks required to use skills by 1%
MAX 30% chance to avoid explosion on Overclock failure. Reduces the number of attacks required to use skills by 20%
Master Kun Level 1 Speed of Spiral of Fire increases by 5%. Increases skill Activation Chance by 0.1%
MAX Speed of Spiral of Fire increases by 50%. Increases skill Activation Chance by 5%
Verdee Level 1 Ultimate cooldown reduce by ?%, skill activation chance increased by ?%
MAX Ultimate cooldown reduce by 50%, skill activation chance increased by 5%
Penguin Musician Level 1 Variation skill has a 1% chance to trigger a better performance. Increase skill activation chance by 0.1%
MAX Variation skill has a 20% chance to trigger a better performance. Increase skill activation chance by 5%

Credit to Yosh and Jubilent for gathering data about the treasure system, who gathered the information and posted it all on the discord before I was able to add it here.
