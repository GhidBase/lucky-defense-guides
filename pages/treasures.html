List of Exclusive Treasures

These are all the unique treasures that only their specific mythic can unlock:

Treasure Level Description
NinjaLevel 1Increase Ninjutsu's skill DMG by 10%. Reduces CD by 1%.
NinjaMAXIncrease Ninjutsu's skill DMG by 100%. Reduces CD by 25%.
Iron MeowLevel 1Increases Iron Meow v2's skill DMG by 5%. Increases CRIT rate by 1%.
Iron MeowMAXIncreases Iron Meow v2's skill DMG by 50%. Increases CRIT rate by 10%.
VayneLevel 1Deals 50% ATK DMG to nearby enemy upon enemy kill. DMG increases by 1% for 3 seconds after moving.
VayneMAXDeals 200% ATK DMG to nearby enemy upon enemy kill. DMG increases by 25% for 3 seconds after moving.
WattLevel 15% energy returned after all energy is used. Increases ATK SPD by 1%.
WattMAX50% energy returned after all energy is used. Increases ATK SPD by 10%.
DragonLevel 1Increases Flame DMG by 5% after using Drain's skill. Increases skill DMG by 1%.
DragonMAXIncreases Flame DMG by 50% after using Drain's skill. Increases skill DMG by 20%.
Lazy TaoistLevel 1Increases Razorwind duration by 0.1 secs. Increases ATK SPD by 1%.
Lazy TaoistMax LevelIncreases Razorwind duration by 0.5 secs. Increases ATK SPD by 10%.
FrogLevel 15% chance to refund 1 random material on each failed curse lift. Increases ULT Damage by 1%.
FrogMAX70% chance to refund 1 random material on each failed curse lift. Increases ULT Damage by 25%.
TarLevel 11% chance to summon a tier 3 Tar when summoning Tar that is not tier 3. Increases skill Activation Chance by 0.1%.
TarMAX30% chance to summon a tier 3 Tar when summoning Tar that is not tier 3. Increases skill Activation Chance by 4%.
MamaLevel 11% chance to summon an imp upon kill with Fear Skill. Increases skill DMG by 1%.
MamaMAX30% chance to summon an imp upon kill with Fear Skill. Increases skill DMG by 20%.
BombaLevel 1Increases Bomba's basic attack DMG by 5%. Increases physical DMG by 1%.
BombaMAXIncreases Bomba's basic attack DMG by 70%. Increases physical DMG by 20%.
Kitty MageLevel 1Increases magic DMG by 1% for 3 seconds after mana recovery. Increases skill Activation Chance by 0.1%.
Kitty MageMAXIncreases magic DMG by 15% for 3 seconds after mana recovery. Increases skill Activation Chance by 5%.
BatmanLevel 1Increases DMG by 1% for 10 seconds upon successful upgrade. Increases CRIT rate by 1%.
BatmanMAXIncreases DMG by 30% for 10 seconds upon successful upgrade. Increases CRIT rate by 10%.
Monopoly ManLevel 1Gains 0.1% of the used MP after an ULT. Recovers 1% of MP at the start of a wave.
Monopoly ManMAXGains 5% of the used MP after an ULT. Recovers 20% of MP at the start of a wave.
BlobLevel 1Increases Devour Count by 5 upon Boss Kill. Increases ATK by 1%.
BlobMAXIncreases Devour Count by 50 upon Boss Kill. Increases ATK by 20%.
IndyLevel 11% chance to upgrade to a higher-tier treasure upon excavation. Increases MOV SPD by 5%.
IndyMAX30% chance to upgrade to a higher-tier treasure upon excavation. Increases MOV SPD by 80%.
LancelotLevel 1Summon Lancelot with 1 Flame level. Increases MP regen by 1%.
LancelotMAXSummon Lancelot with 10 Flame level. Increases MP regen by 30%.
Pulse GeneratorLevel 1Decreases MOV SPD during Space-Time Control by 5%. Increases ULT Damage by 1%.
Pulse GeneratorMAXDecreases MOV SPD during Space-Time Control by 50%. Increases ULT Damage by 25%.
Orc ShamanLevel 1Increases physical DMG taken by curse-stunned enemies by 1% for 3 seconds. Increases skill Activation Chance by 0.1%.
Orc ShamanMAXIncreases physical DMG taken by curse-stunned enemies by 18% for 8 seconds. Increases skill Activation Chance by 5%.
ColdyLevel 1Increases number of Glaciers for Glacier Collision by 3. Increases MP regen by 1%.
ColdyMAXIncreases number of Glaciers for Glacier Collision by 20. Increases MP regen by 30%.
GravitronLevel 1Increases Gravity Control DMG by 10%. Increases skill Activation Chance by 0.1%.
GravitronMAXIncreases Gravity Control DMG by 100%. Increases skill Activation Chance by 5%.
