Note from GhidWard: If you don't have Lancelot level 12, you can still clear hard mode. You'll need level 6 Lancelot at least. Sometimes you'll fall short of doing all the damage needed to clear the boss on wave 80 without having Lancelot level 12 though. The solution to this, is Vayne level 6. If you can get 100k gold, Money Gun can help Vayne do just enough damage to help you finish off the boss. Money Gun and Safe Box level 3 are strongly reccomended in trying to make this work though. Good luck!
