Mythic Upgrade Costs

Upgrading mythics costs mythic stones and gold. These are the costs for each level:

  • To level 2: 5 stones + 1k gold
  • To level 3: 10 stones + 2k gold
  • To level 4: 20 stones + 4k gold
  • To level 5: 30 stones + 6k gold
  • To level 6: 50 stones + 10k gold
  • To level 7: 70 stones + 14k gold
  • To level 8: 100 stones + 20k gold
  • To level 9: 130 stones + 26k gold
  • To level 10: 170 stones + 34k gold
  • To level 11: 210 stones + 42k gold
  • To level 12: 260 stones + 52k gold
  • To level 13: 310 stones + 62k gold
  • To level 14: 360 stones + 72k gold
  • To level 15: 430 stones + 86k gold

Only upgrading for skill unlocks/maxxing, the cost would look like this

  • Level 1 to level 6 costs 115 stones + 23k gold
  • Level 6 to level 12 costs 940 stones + 188k gold
  • Level 12 to level 15 costs 1100 stones + 220k gold
